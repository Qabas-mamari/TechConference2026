About the Conference

International Conference on Technology & Innovation in Healthcare - Muscat 2026

The International Conference on Technology & Innovation in Healthcare brings together global experts, policymakers, and innovators to explore cutting-edge advancements in digital health, artificial intelligence, and medical technology. The conference aims to foster collaboration and drive transformation in healthcare delivery and innovation across Oman and beyond.

  • 2 days of interactive sessions and technology showcases
  • Renowned speakers and healthcare innovators from around the world
  • Workshops on AI, digital transformation, and smart health systems
  • Networking opportunities for professionals, researchers, and investors

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the International Conference on Technology & Innovation in Healthcare – Muscat 2026?
This conference brings together healthcare professionals, innovators, and technology experts to explore the latest advancements in healthcare technology, digital health, and medical innovation. It features keynote speeches, workshops, and networking opportunities.
When and where will the event be held?
The conference will take place on **January 19–20, 2026** in **Muscat, Sultanate of Oman**.
How can I register for the conference?
You can register through our official website by filling out the registration form to secure your spot at the conference.
Who should attend this conference?
Healthcare professionals, researchers, technology innovators, policymakers, and students interested in healthcare technology and digital innovation are encouraged to attend.
Will virtual participation be available?
No, this is an in-person event. All participants are expected to attend in Muscat.
Will certificates of participation be provided?
Yes, all registered attendees will receive a certificate of participation after the event.
